Nigeria's Integrated National
Financing Framework
Monitoring & Coordination Platform
This platform is Nigeria integrated national system for tracking, analysing, and coordinating financing for sustainable development. It brings together financing flows, reforms, and results under the INFF — aligning investments with national priorities and the SDGs. Designed as a living system, it supports interactive analysis, on-demand reporting, and transparent communication across government, development partners, and the private sector.
